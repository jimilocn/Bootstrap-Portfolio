Jimi Loc Nguyen hails from the lower peninsula of Mitten State. Growing up in a small farming community near Grand Rapids, Jimi activated his love for manifesting his colorful imagination as an avid musician. In his primary education, Jimi became well practiced in Flute, Clarinet, Saxophone, and Violin.

Throughout his years , he transitioned his efforts from music composition and arrangement, to material arts, graphic design, and even movement and dance choreography. Today, Jimi works as a Chiropractic technician at The Chiro One Wellness Center of Andersonville by day, and an overly enthusiastic coder by night at Northwestern University in Evanston. He is excited for his trajectory forward into the world of technology and computer sciences.

With his rigorous background in dance and the arts, Jimi Loc Nguyen is the perfect storm of grit, passion, and creativity. Website design is the new vehicle for his path forward, but with all the tools that he has acquired in life, he is destined to flourish and succeed.
